NOCTA

Wooden chairs 2024

Dimension: 531 x 508 x 704 cm

The Nocta chair is a modern reinterpretation of a Scandinavian-inspired mid-back bridge chair. Crafting a design with such organic lines demands true mastery of 3D modeling tools. The chair’s signature feature lies in its legs: their square profile, visible from the outside, is defined by sharp, prominent edges that create a precise, sculpted silhouette. On the inside, these edges soften into a rounded form, producing a smooth and natural transition to the seat and backrest. In this way, the chair juxtaposes taut, assertive exterior lines with gentle, enveloping interior contours. This contrast gives it a distinctive identity and offers a fresh perspective on a design that is often copied and adapted.
