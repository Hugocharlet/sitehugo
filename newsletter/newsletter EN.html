Galerie Joseph / Paris Design Week

Many thanks to Paris Design Week Factory for allowing me to exhibit my latest creation: a collection of armchairs with fluid, harmonious lines, evoking both Japanese and Scandinavian inspirations.

Hugo Charlet & JL_Studio / PIXA chair

We are also pleased to present the PIXA chair, designed by Hugo Charlet and published by the young brand JL_studio. This chair stands out for its radical, sculptural and brutalist design. But the more you look at it, the more you discover its endearing, playful character, giving it a timeless charm.

Company : JL_ Studio site

Exhibition from September 5 to 9 Galerie Joseph, 7 Rue Froissart 75003 Paris.
