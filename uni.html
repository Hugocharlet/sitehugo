UNI Armchair

Wooden chairs 2026

Dimension: 531 x 508 x 704 cm

The Uni armchair draws its inspiration from a model called the University Chair. Its lines are clean, radical, and deliberately rigorous, while maintaining a cool elegance. Curves and organic shapes give way to facets oriented at 0, 45, or 90 degrees. I wanted to use beech for the frame, an extremely hard and smooth wood. This choice of material naturally led me to these striking and radical lines.
